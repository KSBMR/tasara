Our Products

Polypropylene (PP)

Polypropylene (PP) is a versatile thermoplastic polymer widely used in various industries due to its excellent properties. It is made from the polymerization of propylene monomers and is known for its lightweight, chemical resistance, and durability.

Polystyrene(PS)

Polystyrene (PS) is a versatile, synthetic polymer made from styrene monomers. It is a widely used plastic due to its lightweight, insulating properties, and ease of molding.

Polyethylene (PE)

Polyethylene (PE) is one of the most widely used and versatile thermoplastic polymers. It is composed of long chains of ethylene monomers (C₂H₄), making it lightweight, durable, and chemically resistant.
